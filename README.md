**Project Objective:**

Develop an **interactive Power BI dashboard** for **Shield Insurance** to analyze key business metrics such as revenue, customer segments, policies, and claims.

Support the Sales, Marketing, and Operations teams in making **data-driven decision**s by improving visibility into performance metrics and customer behavior.

**Purpose of Analysis:**

To enable the organization to **track** financial and operational performance, identify profitable customer segments, and optimize sales channels through data insights.

**Importance of Analytics in Insurance:**

Identify **revenue-driving products** and **customer segments.**

Analyze claim settlement ratios and policy **growth trends.**

Detect sales opportunities through **channel** and **regional performance.**

**Role of Reports:**
Track **KPIs** such as Revenue Growth, Customer Retention, and Claim Settlement Ratios.

Provide **actionable insights** by age group, city, and sales mode (online app, website, offline, direct).

Improve decision-making across **multiple** business departments.

**Technical & Soft Skills:**

**Technical Skills:**

Integrated **10+ datasets** including customers, policies, claims, and channels.

Performed data cleaning and transformation using **Power Query.**

Created DAX measures for **KPIs** like revenue growth, customer segmentation, and claim ratios.

**Built interactive visuals** using bookmarks, slicers, and filters.

Designed role-based views for **stakeholder-specific** reporting.

Published live reports to Power BI Service for **cross-team** accessibility.

**Soft Skills:**

**Collaborated with multiple business teams** to refine dashboard insights.

Simplified complex data into clear, actionable visuals.

Strong communication and presentation of analytical findings.

Applied problem-solving and structured analytical thinking.

**Key Insights & Outcomes:**

Revealed key revenue and customer behavior trends by **city and age group.**

Highlighted performance variations across **sales channels.**

Provided insights that led to a **10%** increase in active users and a **15%** growth in profit.

Improved operational efficiency and stakeholder understanding through a user-friendly dashboard.

**Outcome:**

Delivered a **comprehensive Power BI dashboard** for Shield Insurance that combined **data modeling,** visualization, and **performance tracking,** helping the company adopt a **data-driven strategy** for growth and operational excellence.
